ABOUT
M&S

Marque and Synergy International Limited (M&S) is an oil and gas trading company established since 2001 in Abuja, Nigeria, with a network of key relationships in over 20 countries in Africa.

Over the years, M&S has specialized in oil and gas trade and consultancy as a result of long established strategic partnerships, industry expertise and a vast network of contacts in NOC’s, IOC’s, Refineries and key local players in the oil and gas industry in various African nations.

M&S has about 40 years of combined experience in trading, gas and power, finance and upstream asset acquisition.

OUR
SERVICES

UPSTREAM

The upstream industry in sub-Saharan Africa is driven firstly by political will and secondly by professional competence (technical, commercial and financial). This usually creates the perception of a complex jigsaw puzzle for outside would-be investors. M&S is strategically located with the right relationships thereby providing not so available knowledge of viable and relevant upstream assets, putting us at an advantage which ensures successful upstream venture.

CONSULTANCY

With our global partnerships and in-depth knowledge of the sub-Saharan energy sector, we provide long term innovative solutions to various problems associated with the Energy market.

TRADE

M&S Trading provides requisite skills and experience to make split second decisions that ensure financial risk management and sound business judgement in a market that is constantly changing due to global fluctuations in demand and supply.

LOGISTICS - SHIPPING

We provide relevant cost saving solutions for the movement and storage of petroleum products with around-the-clock support tailored to ensure all shipments are delivered to their destination safely and on schedule.

LOGISTICS - TERMINALS

We offer clients a comprehensive terminal management package in cases of regulatory compliance unforeseen circumstances relating to vessel maintenance and other operating procedures.
