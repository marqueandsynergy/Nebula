About
M&S

Marque and Synergy International Limited (M&S) is a leading oil and gas trading company established in 2001 in Abuja, Nigeria, with a network spanning over 20 African countries.

With decades of expertise, M&S specializes in oil and gas trade and consultancy, leveraging strategic partnerships, industry knowledge, and connections with NOCs, IOCs, refineries, and key players across Africa.

M&S brings 40 years of combined experience in trading, gas and power, finance, and upstream asset acquisition, delivering innovative energy solutions.

Our Vision
