About M&S

Marque and Synergy International Limited (M&S), founded in 2001 in Abuja, Nigeria, is a premier oil and gas trading company with a network spanning over 20 African countries. Explore our services.

With strategic partnerships with NOCs, IOCs, refineries, and key industry players, M&S specializes in oil and gas trade, consultancy, upstream asset acquisition, and infrastructure projects like jetties and tankage across sub-Saharan Africa.

Boasting 40 years of combined experience, M&S delivers innovative solutions in trading, gas and power, finance, and logistics, driving sustainable growth in the African energy sector. Contact us to learn more.

ABOUT M&S

Marque and Synergy International Limited (M&S) is an oil and gas trading company established in 2001 in Abuja, Nigeria, with a network of key relationships in over 20 African countries.

Over the years, M&S has specialized in oil and gas trade and consultancy through long-established strategic partnerships, industry expertise, and a vast network of contacts in NOCs, IOCs, refineries, and key players in the African oil and gas industry.

M&S brings over 40 years of combined experience in trading, gas and power, finance, and upstream asset acquisition.

OUR VISION & MISSION

VISION

To make a significant impact in the African energy industry and influence change in the way energy is traded.

MISSION

To provide innovative solutions to Africa's energy challenges by leveraging strategic relationships, relevant data, market intelligence, and available resources.
